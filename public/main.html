Frequently Asked Questions

A smart education website uses advanced technologies to provide interactive, personalized, and efficient learning experiences for students and teachers.
To enroll in a course, simply log in to your account, navigate to the 'Courses' section, and select the course you wish to enroll in. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your enrollment.
If you forget your password, click on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page. You will be prompted to enter your email address, and a password reset link will be sent to you.
Assignments can be submitted through the 'Assignments' section in your course dashboard. Follow the instructions provided to upload and submit your assignment before the deadline.
You can track your performance through the 'Performance' section on your dashboard. This section provides detailed analytics and reports on your progress and grades.
